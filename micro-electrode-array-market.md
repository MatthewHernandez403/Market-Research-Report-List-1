<p><ul><li>Classical MEA</li><li>Multiwell-MEA</li><li>CMOS-MEA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Micro Electrode Array (MEA) market comprises three primary types: Classical MEA, Multiwell-MEA, and CMOS-MEA. Classical MEAs feature a simple and compact design, suitable for basic electrophysiological recordings. Multiwell-MEAs expand this concept, offering multiple compartments for higher throughput, enabling parallel experiments. CMOS-MEAs utilize advanced complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, providing enhanced sensitivity and integration capabilities, making them ideal for complex neural and cellular studies. Together, these MEA types facilitate various applications in neuroscience, drug discovery, and tissue engineering.</p></p>
