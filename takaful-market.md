<p><p>Takaful markets are categorized into two main types: Life/Family Takaful and General Takaful. Life/Family Takaful focuses on protection and savings plans, providing coverage for participants against risks associated with life, such as death or disability, while also offering investment opportunities. Conversely, General Takaful covers non-life risks, including property, health, and liability, protecting participants against unforeseen events. Both types operate on principles of mutual cooperation, where participants contribute to a shared fund to support one another in times of need.</p></p>
