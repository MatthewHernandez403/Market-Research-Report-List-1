<p><strong>The Ceramic Electro Static Chuck Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ceramic Electro Static Chuck (ESC) market is primarily divided into two types: Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type. Coulomb Type ESCs use electrostatic forces to hold substrates during semiconductor processing, providing uniformity and excellent thermal control. On the other hand, JR Type ESCs employ a different mechanism, utilizing a combination of attractive electrostatic forces and mechanical clamping, offering stability in high-vacuum environments. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing wafer processing efficiency and precision in advanced manufacturing settings.</p></p>
