<p><p>The multifunction massage machine market is primarily divided into two types: numerical control massage machines and mechanical massage machines. Numerical control massage machines utilize advanced technology and programmable settings to deliver precise and customizable massage experiences, often incorporating various therapies such as kneading and tapping for enhanced relaxation. In contrast, mechanical massage machines employ traditional methods, using physical mechanisms to simulate human touch. This makes them typically more straightforward and accessible, catering to users seeking basic massage relief without intricate features.</p></p>
