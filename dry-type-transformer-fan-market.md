<p><p>The Dry-type Transformer Fan Market is categorized into two primary types: Top Blowing Type and Side Blowing Type. The Top Blowing Type directs airflow from the top, efficiently cooling the transformer while minimizing space requirements. It is ideal for installations with height constraints. Conversely, the Side Blowing Type expels air from the sides, offering enhanced cooling for larger transformers and installations with adequate lateral space. Both types are essential for maintaining optimal operating temperatures in dry-type transformers.</p></p>
