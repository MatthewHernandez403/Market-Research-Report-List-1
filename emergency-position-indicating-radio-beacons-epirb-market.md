<p><p>Emergency Position Indicating Radio Beacons (EPIRBs) are crucial for maritime safety, designed to transmit distress signals to search and rescue authorities during emergencies. The market is divided into two main types: automatic and manual. Automatic EPIRBs activate upon immersion in water, providing immediate alerts, making them ideal for boats that may capsize. Manual EPIRBs require user activation and are suited for situations where individuals can signal emergencies directly. Both types are essential for enhancing safety at sea and ensuring rapid rescue responses.</p></p>
