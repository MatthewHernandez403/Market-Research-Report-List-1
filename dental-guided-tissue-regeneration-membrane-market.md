<p><p>The Dental Guided Tissue Regeneration Membrane market is segmented into absorbable and non-absorbable membranes. Absorbable membranes are designed to be gradually absorbed by the body, eliminating the need for a second surgery for removal. They are made from materials like collagen or polymers, promoting tissue healing. In contrast, non-absorbable membranes are made from materials such as titanium or dense polymers, providing long-term support but requiring surgical removal after the healing process. Both types play crucial roles in dental regeneration procedures.</p></p>
