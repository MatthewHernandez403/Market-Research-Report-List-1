<p><p>The Meso-Micro Porous Carbon Materials market is categorized into two primary types based on pore size: microporous materials, which possess pores less than 2 nm, and mesoporous materials, characterized by pores ranging from 2 to 50 nm. Microporous materials are typically used in applications like gas separation and catalysis, offering high surface area and selectivity. In contrast, mesoporous materials provide improved accessibility for larger molecules, making them suitable for drug delivery, energy storage, and environmental remediation solutions.</p></p>
