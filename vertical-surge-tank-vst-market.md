<p><p>Vertical Surge Tanks (VST) are crucial in regulating pressure fluctuations in water supply and hydropower systems. The market for VSTs is segmented into Single Compartment and Twin Compartment types. Single Compartment VSTs provide a simple design suitable for smaller systems, managing pressure variations effectively. In contrast, Twin Compartment VSTs feature two interconnected chambers, offering enhanced stability and response to rapid flow changes, making them ideal for larger and more complex systems. Both designs optimize performance and prolong infrastructure lifespan.</p></p>
