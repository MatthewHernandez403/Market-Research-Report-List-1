<p><p>The food contact paper market is primarily divided into two types: non-poly-coated and poly-coated. Non-poly-coated papers are typically uncoated, allowing for better breathability but may offer limited moisture resistance. These are often used for items like bakery goods. In contrast, poly-coated papers are treated with a polyethylene layer, enhancing moisture, grease, and oil resistance, making them suitable for packaging items like take-out containers and frozen foods. Both types serve distinct purposes in food safety and preservation.</p></p>
