<p><p>Filtration fabrics are classified into several types based on their weave structure. Monolayer weave offers a single-layer barrier that allows for efficient filtration of particulates, while double weave combines two layers for enhanced strength and filtration capability. Three-layer weave provides superior filtration by incorporating multiple materials, ensuring optimal performance in diverse applications. Additionally, the "Other" category includes specialized fabrics with unique weaves or enhancements tailored for specific filtration needs, catering to varying industry demands such as water treatment, air purification, and industrial processes.</p></p>
