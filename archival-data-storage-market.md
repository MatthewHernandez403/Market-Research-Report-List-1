<p><p>Archival data storage encompasses two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise storage keeps data within an organization's physical infrastructure, providing direct control and security but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. Organizations often choose between these options based on their specific data needs, budget constraints, and regulatory compliance requirements, balancing control against convenience.</p></p>
