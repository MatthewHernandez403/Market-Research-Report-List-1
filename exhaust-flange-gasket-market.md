<p><p>The exhaust flange gasket market features various types designed to create airtight seals in exhaust systems. Tapered rings provide a tight fit and good sealing under high temperatures. Crush rings, made from soft materials, deform under pressure to fill gaps effectively. Steel rings offer durability and resistance to extreme conditions, ensuring longevity. Steel mesh rings combine strength with flexibility, capable of withstanding vibration and thermal expansion. Each type caters to specific engine requirements, enhancing performance and reliability in exhaust systems.</p></p>
