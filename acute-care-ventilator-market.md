<p><p>The Acute Care Ventilator market is categorized into three main types: Electronic, Pneumatic, and Electro-Pneumatic ventilators. Electronic ventilators utilize advanced software and sensors for precise control and monitoring, making them suitable for complex patient needs. Pneumatic ventilators rely on mechanical systems powered by compressed air, offering simplicity and reliability in emergency situations. Electro-Pneumatic ventilators combine both electronic controls and pneumatic mechanisms, enabling versatile functionality to cater to a wide range of clinical scenarios and patient requirements.</p></p>
