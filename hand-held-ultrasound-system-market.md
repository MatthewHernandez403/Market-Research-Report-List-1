<p><p>The handheld ultrasound system market comprises various types, including B-scan ultrasonography, color ultrasound, and others. B-scan ultrasonography provides two-dimensional images and is commonly used for structural examinations. Color ultrasound enhances B-scan images by incorporating color flow Doppler technology, allowing visualization of blood flow and tissue movement. Other types may include 3D/4D imaging and elastography, which further expand diagnostic capabilities. These diverse modalities cater to various clinical needs, improving patient outcomes through accessible imaging solutions.</p></p>
