<p><p>Content Management Systems (CMS) are software platforms that facilitate the creation, management, and publishing of digital content. The market is divided into two primary types: web-based and on-premises. Web-based CMS are hosted in the cloud, offering flexibility, automatic updates, and easy collaboration, making them suitable for businesses of all sizes. On-premises CMS, however, are installed locally on a company's servers, providing enhanced control, security, and customization, making them ideal for organizations with specific compliance or performance requirements.</p></p>
