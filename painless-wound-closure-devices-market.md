<p><p>The Painless Wound Closure Devices market consists of three primary types: closure strips, tissue adhesives, and sutures. Closure strips are adhesive strips that approximate the edges of a wound without penetrating the skin, promoting healing with minimal pain. Tissue adhesives are liquid adhesives that bond tissues together, offering a painless alternative to sutures. Sutures, while traditionally used for wound closure, have evolved to include innovative materials and designs aimed at reducing discomfort during the closure process.</p></p>
