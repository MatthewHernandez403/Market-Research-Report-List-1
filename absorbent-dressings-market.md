<p><strong>The Absorbent Dressings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Adherent</li><li>Adherent</li><li>Low-Adherent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Absorbent dressings are crucial for wound management, categorized into three main types. Non-adherent dressings prevent sticking to the wound, minimizing pain during a dressing change and allowing for easier removal. Adherent dressings stick to the wound bed, promoting optimal healing but may cause discomfort upon removal. Low-adherent dressings offer a balance, lightly sticking to the wound to maintain contact without excessive adherence, facilitating easier changes while still providing necessary absorption and protection. Each type serves specific wound needs effectively.</p></p>
