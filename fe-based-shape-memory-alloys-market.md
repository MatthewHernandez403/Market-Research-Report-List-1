<p><ul><li>High Temperature Austenite Phase</li><li>Low Temperature Martensite Phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fe-based shape memory alloys market is categorized into high-temperature austenite phase and low-temperature martensite phase. High-temperature austenite phase alloys exhibit shape memory effects at elevated temperatures, making them suitable for applications in industrial sectors, such as aerospace and automotive, where thermal resistance is critical. Conversely, low-temperature martensite phase alloys function well at lower temperatures, making them ideal for medical devices and cryogenic applications. Both types leverage unique transformation properties to enhance performance across various industries.</p></p>
