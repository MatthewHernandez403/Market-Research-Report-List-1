<p><p>The Motorcycle Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) Sensors market is primarily categorized into two types: the Attitude and Heading Reference System (AHRS) and the Inertial Navigation System (INS). AHRS provides precise orientation and heading data, crucial for stability and control in motorcycle dynamics, while INS calculates the vehicle's position and velocity over time by integrating acceleration data. Both systems enhance rider safety and navigation accuracy, playing a significant role in modern motorcycle design and functionality.</p></p>
