<p><p>The Medical Thermoplastic Polyurethane Elastomer (TPU) market is segmented into types based on chemical composition: Polyester-Based, Polyether-Based, and Others. Polyester-Based TPUs offer high strength and abrasion resistance, making them suitable for applications like surgical devices and implants. Polyether-Based TPUs provide enhanced flexibility and hydrolytic stability, ideal for moisture-prone environments in medical supplies. The "Others" category includes specialized formulations tailored for specific medical applications, further expanding the versatility of TPU materials in healthcare settings.</p></p>
