<p><p>The luminescent paint market comprises three main types: **fluorescent paint**, which emits visible light when exposed to ultraviolet rays; **phosphorescent paint**, which absorbs light and then re-emits it over time, creating a glow-in-the-dark effect; and **radio luminescent paint**, which contains radioactive materials to produce light. Each type serves different applications, including safety signage, art, and decorative purposes, offering unique properties for visibility and aesthetics in various environments.</p></p>
