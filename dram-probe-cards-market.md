<p><p>The DRAM probe cards market is divided into two main types: MEMS probe cards and Non-MEMS probe cards. MEMS probe cards leverage micro-electromechanical systems technology, offering higher precision and scalability, making them suitable for advanced packaging and testing. In contrast, Non-MEMS probe cards are traditional options, often using metallic probes and providing reliability for standard testing applications. The choice between these types depends on specific testing requirements, performance needs, and cost considerations in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
