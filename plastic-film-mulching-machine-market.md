<p><ul><li>Compression Type</li><li>Embedded Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic film mulching machine market is categorized into two primary types: compression type and embedded type. Compression type machines apply a layer of plastic film over soil using pressure, effectively enhancing soil temperature, moisture retention, and weed control. In contrast, embedded type machines integrate the plastic film directly into the soil, promoting soil health and reducing surface runoff. Both types serve the common purpose of improving crop yield and sustainability in agricultural practices.</p></p>
