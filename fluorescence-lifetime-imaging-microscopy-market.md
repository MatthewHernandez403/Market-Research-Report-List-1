<p><strong>The Fluorescence Lifetime Imaging Microscopy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Time-Domain</li><li>Frequency-Domain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluorescence Lifetime Imaging Microscopy (FLIM) is categorized into Time-Domain and Frequency-Domain techniques. Time-Domain FLIM measures the lifetime of fluorescence signals using time-correlated single-photon counting, capturing rapid dynamics in biological samples. Conversely, Frequency-Domain FLIM employs modulation of excitation light and analyzes the phase shift between the excitation and emission signals, allowing for efficient measurements in highly scattering samples. Both methods enhance imaging quality and provide insights into molecular environments, contributing significantly to research in cellular dynamics and biological processes.</p></p>
