HDL cholesterol strips specifically measure high-density lipoprotein, often known as "good" cholesterol, which plays a crucial role in cardiovascular health. Triglycerides strips, on the other hand, evaluate triglyceride levels, which can indicate the risk of heart disease. Together, these strips empower individuals to monitor their lipid profiles effectively for better health management.</p></p>
