<p><p>The cancer diagnostics market can be categorized into platform-based and instrument-based markets. Platform-based diagnostics involve comprehensive systems that integrate various assays and technologies, enabling multi-faceted analyses of cancer biomarkers, facilitating personalized treatment approaches. Conversely, instrument-based diagnostics focus on specific tools or devices, such as imaging or histopathology equipment, that aid in the direct detection or evaluation of cancer. Both categories play crucial roles in improving diagnosis accuracy and supporting effective treatment decisions in oncology.</p></p>
