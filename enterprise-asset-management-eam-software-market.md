<p><p>Enterprise Asset Management (EAM) software is categorized into two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise EAM solutions are installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring significant upfront investments and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based EAM software is hosted remotely, providing flexibility, scalability, and reduced IT overhead. This model enables easier access and collaboration, as users can access the system from anywhere with an internet connection, streamlining asset management processes.</p></p>
