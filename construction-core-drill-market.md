<p><p>The construction core drill market features two primary types of drill bits: wet drill bits and dry drill bits. Wet drill bits utilize water or coolant to reduce heat and improve efficiency when drilling through hard materials, making them suitable for concrete and masonry applications. Conversely, dry drill bits operate without any coolant, generating less mess but producing more heat, which may limit their use to softer materials or shorter drilling tasks. Both types offer unique advantages depending on project requirements.</p></p>
