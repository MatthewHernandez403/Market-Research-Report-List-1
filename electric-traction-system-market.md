<p><p>The Electric Traction System market is primarily categorized into two types: Alternating Current (AC) Supply and Direct Current (DC) Supply. AC supply is commonly used for high-speed and long-distance rail systems due to its efficiency over long distances and lower transmission losses. In contrast, DC supply is typically utilized in urban transit systems, such as subways and trams, offering simpler technology and more effective energy management at lower speeds. Each type serves distinct applications based on operational requirements and infrastructure design.</p></p>
