<p><p>The 5G Pico Base Station market is divided into two main types: Stand Alone (SA) and Non-Stand Alone (NSA). SA architecture operates independently of previous generations, utilizing a 5G core for enhanced performance and low latency. In contrast, NSA architecture relies on existing 4G infrastructure, allowing for a quicker deployment while still leveraging some 5G capabilities. Both types aim to increase network density, support IoT applications, and provide coverage in urban areas, yet differ in their implementation and infrastructure dependencies.</p></p>
