<p><ul><li>Active Cold Chain</li><li>Passive Cold Chain</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biopharmaceutical cold chain logistics market is categorized into three main types. Active cold chain systems use temperature-controlled equipment such as refrigerated trucks or containers to maintain consistent temperatures during transport. Passive cold chain systems rely on insulated packaging and temperature-stabilizing materials without mechanical assistance, catering to shorter timeframes or less sensitive products. Additionally, the "Others" category includes innovative temperature management solutions, advanced tracking technologies, and hybrid systems that combine elements of both active and passive methods for enhanced logistics efficiency.</p></p>
