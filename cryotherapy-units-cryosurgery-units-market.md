<p><ul><li>Liquid Nitrogen Therapy</li><li>Dry Ice Therapy</li><li>Electric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cryotherapy units, commonly used in cryosurgery, include various types such as liquid nitrogen therapy, dry ice therapy, and electric cryotherapy. Liquid nitrogen therapy utilizes extremely low temperatures to freeze and destroy targeted tissues, making it effective for treating skin lesions and tumors. Dry ice therapy employs solid carbon dioxide to achieve localized cooling, suitable for minor dermatological procedures. Electric cryotherapy units use electrical mechanisms to achieve freezing temperatures, offering precision in treatment while minimizing collateral damage to surrounding tissues.</p></p>
