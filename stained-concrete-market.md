<p><ul><li>Acid-based Staining</li><li>Water-based Staining</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stained concrete market is primarily divided into two types: acid-based staining and water-based staining. Acid-based staining uses chemical reactions with the concrete to create vibrant, translucent colors that penetrate deeply, resulting in a natural, variegated look. It is often preferred for its durability and rich hues. On the other hand, water-based staining offers a wider range of colors and finishes, is environmentally friendly, and provides a more uniform appearance, making it suitable for residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
