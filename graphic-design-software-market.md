<p><strong>The Graphic Design Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pixel-based Image Editors</li><li>Vector-based Image Editors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The graphic design software market is primarily divided into pixel-based and vector-based image editors. Pixel-based image editors, like Adobe Photoshop, manipulate images as a grid of individual pixels, making them ideal for detailed photo editing and raster graphics. In contrast, vector-based image editors, such as Adobe Illustrator, use mathematical formulas to create images composed of paths, which allows for infinite scalability without loss of quality, making them perfect for logos and illustrations. Both types serve distinct creative needs in design.</p></p>
