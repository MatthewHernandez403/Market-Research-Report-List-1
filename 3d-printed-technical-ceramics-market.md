<p><p>The 3D printed technical ceramics market can be segmented into two key types based on material deposition methods: material deposited and liquid deposition. Material deposited techniques involve the layer-by-layer application of ceramic powders, enabling precise control over geometries and properties. Conversely, liquid deposition methods utilize ceramic suspensions to build structures through processes like inkjet or direct ink writing. Both methods cater to diverse applications, including aerospace, dental, and automotive sectors, enhancing design flexibility and material performance in advanced manufacturing.</p></p>
