<p><p>Oily Water Separators (OWS) are essential for treating bilge water in maritime operations, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations. The market includes three primary types: </p><p>1. **Mechanical Based**: Utilizes physical separation techniques like gravity and coalescence to remove oil from water. </p><p>2. **Chemical Based**: Employs chemicals to enhance separation, improving oil recovery and reducing residual contaminants. </p><p>3. **Membrane Based**: Uses semi-permeable membranes to selectively filter oils from water, offering high-performance separation while minimizing wastewater. </p><p>Each type serves distinct applications and efficiencies.</p></p>
