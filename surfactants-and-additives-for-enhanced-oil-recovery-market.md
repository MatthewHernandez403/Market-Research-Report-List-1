Common types include anionic, cationic, and nonionic surfactants, each with specific applications. Additives, such as polymeric agents and corrosion inhibitors, enhance the performance of EOR processes by maintaining viscosity, preventing equipment degradation, and optimizing fluid properties. Together, these components play a crucial role in maximizing oil recovery.</p></p>
