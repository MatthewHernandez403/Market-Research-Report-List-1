<p><p>Veneer dentistry market primarily consists of two main types: composite materials and dental porcelain. Composite veneers are made from tooth-colored resin, offering a less expensive, minimally invasive option that can be applied in a single visit. However, they may stain over time. On the other hand, dental porcelain veneers are more durable and aesthetically pleasing, resembling natural teeth while providing greater resistance to wear and staining. Both types cater to patients seeking aesthetic improvements in their smiles.</p></p>
