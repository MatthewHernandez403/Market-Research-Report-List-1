<p><p>The water-based lithium-ion battery binder market consists of two main types: anode binders and cathode binders. Anode binders, used in the negative electrode, are essential for enhancing the adhesion of active materials and improving battery performance. Cathode binders, utilized in the positive electrode, serve similar purposes, ensuring stability and conductivity within the electrode structure. The shift towards water-based formulations in both segments aims to reduce environmental impact, enhance safety, and improve manufacturing efficiency in battery production.</p></p>
