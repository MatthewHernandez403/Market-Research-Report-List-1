<p><ul><li>Primers</li><li>Probes</li><li>Intermediate-scale Synthesis Oligos</li><li>Large-scale Synthesis Oligos</li><li>Linkers and Adaptors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DNA oligonucleotide synthesis market comprises various types, including primers, probes, intermediate-scale synthesis oligos, large-scale synthesis oligos, and linkers and adaptors. Primers are short strands used in PCR and sequencing, while probes play a role in detection and quantification. Intermediate-scale synthesis oligos cater to medium-scale applications, whereas large-scale synthesis oligos are designed for bulk production in research and therapeutic use. Linkers and adaptors facilitate the connection of different DNA fragments, enhancing versatility in genetic applications.</p></p>
