<p><p>The induction flush valve market includes two main types: hidden induction flush valves and surface-mounted induction flush valves. Hidden induction flush valves are designed to be discreetly integrated into restroom fixtures, providing a sleek and modern look while offering touchless operation for improved hygiene. In contrast, surface-mounted induction flush valves are installed visibly on walls or fixtures, emphasizing convenience and ease of access. Both types enhance sanitation by minimizing direct contact, catering to the increasing demand for hygienic restroom solutions.</p></p>
